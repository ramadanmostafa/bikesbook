Privay Policy

This Privacy Policy describes Bikesbook™'s policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information. When using any of our Services you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

INFORMATION COLLECTED

When you create an account, you provide some personal information, such as your name, email address, gender and username. You may provide us with additional information and content while using our Services that contain your personal information, such as your location or a picture. Some of this information, such as your name and username, is listed publicly on our Services, including on your profile page and in search results. The majority of our Services are visible to the public and do not require registration to be viewed.

COOKIES

We use "cookies" to make Bikesbook™ easier to use and to help improve our Services. A cookie is used by many websites and is a small data file stored for a period of time on your computer. Cookies help us to understand how you interact with Bikesbook™ and are also used to store your username (but never your password) to make it easier for you to login whenever you come back to Bikesbook™.

LOG DATA

Our servers automatically record information created by your use of our Services, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, interactions with advertisements and search terms.

LINKS

When you click on a link on Bikesbook™ you may leave our site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES AND PROVIDERS

We use a variety of services hosted by third parties and engage certain third party service providers to help provide our Services. These services may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, such as cookies or your IP request and we may share your personal information with these service providers, but only to the extent necessary to perform their services.

VIEWING AND EDITING YOUR PROFILE

If you are a registered user of Bikesbook™, we provide you with the ability to access and modify the personal information you provided to us.

INFORMATION SHARING

We do not disclose your private information except in the limited circumstances outlined in this Privacy Policy.

We may disclose your information if we believe that it is necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request; to protect the safety of any person; to address fraud, security or technical issues; or to protect Bikesbook™'s rights or property.

The rights outlined in the Privacy Policy will continue to apply to your information as transferred to the new entity.

NON-PRIVATE OR NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION
